Bloch Sphere

The general spin $1/2$ state $$ | ψ ⟩ = \cos θ / 2 | ↑ ⟩ + e^{iφ} \sin θ / 2 | ↓ ⟩ $$ suggests a representation on the 3-dimensional unit sphere

State $ | ψ ⟩$ is represented by the point $$ {n}↖{ → } = (\table \sin θ \cos φ; \sin θ \sin φ; \cos θ) $$ on the bloch sphere.
Note: Point on opposite sides correspond to orthogonal states $$ ⟨ ψ _{{n}↖{ → }} | ψ _{{n}↖{ → }} ⟩ = 0 ⟩ $$ Note: The coordinates of ${n}↖{ → }$ correspond to the expectation values of the spin operators $$ {n}↖{ → } = 2 / ℏ ⟨ ψ| S ↖ {\^} | ψ ⟩ = ⟨ ψ| σ ↖ {\^} | ψ ⟩ = (\table ⟨ ψ| σ ↖ {\^} _x| ψ ⟩; ⟨ ψ| σ ↖ {\^} _y | ψ ⟩; ⟨ ψ| σ ↖ {\^} _z| ψ ⟩) $$
